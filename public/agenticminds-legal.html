§ 09

Children's Privacy & COPPA Compliance

AgenticMinds is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children online. This section describes how we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. § 6501 et seq., and the FTC's implementing regulations (16 C.F.R. Part 312).

Not Directed to Children Under 13. Our Services are not directed to, designed for, or intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly market to or solicit personal information from children under 13. Our registration flow includes an age verification step — users who indicate they are under 13 are blocked from creating an account and no data is retained.

No Knowing Collection of Children's Data. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13 without prior verifiable parental consent as required by COPPA. "Personal information" under COPPA includes: name, home address, email address, phone number, Social Security number, persistent identifiers such as cookies and IP addresses, photos or audio files containing a child's image or voice, geolocation data sufficient to identify a street address, and any information that could be used to identify or contact a specific child.

Parental Notification & Verifiable Consent. If we ever offer Services or features accessible to children under 13, we will — before collecting any personal information — provide direct notice to parents or guardians describing what we collect and how it is used, obtain verifiable parental consent via an FTC-approved method (e.g., signed consent form, credit card verification, or government ID verification), and give parents the option to consent to internal use without authorizing third-party disclosure. We will not condition a child's participation on disclosing more information than is reasonably necessary.

Parental Rights. Parents or legal guardians may at any time: review personal information collected from their child; request deletion of that information; refuse further collection or use; and revoke previously granted consent. To exercise these rights, email agenticmindshelp@gmail.com with the subject line "COPPA Parental Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 10 business days.

Third-Party Operators. We contractually require all third-party service providers integrated into our platform to comply with COPPA and prohibit them from collecting personal information from children under 13 through our Services without parental consent.

Cookies & Tracking — Children. We do not use persistent identifiers to track children under 13 for behavioral advertising. If we determine a visitor is under 13, we will immediately cease data collection, delete any associated persistent identifiers, and restrict access to account-creation features.

AI Features & Children. Given the open-ended nature of AI interactions, we apply heightened safeguards to ensure our AI-powered tools are inaccessible to children under 13 without verified parental consent. We do not use data submitted by a child under 13 to train or improve our AI models without explicit parental authorization.

Inadvertent Collection. If we discover we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it, notify the parent or guardian where possible, and refrain from using it for any secondary purpose.

Reporting a COPPA Concern. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to AgenticMinds without parental consent, contact us immediately at agenticmindshelp@gmail.com with the subject line "COPPA — Child Data Concern." You may also file a complaint directly with the FTC at ftc.gov or 1-877-FTC-HELP.

FTC COPPA Resource: For more information about children's online privacy rights, visit ftc.gov/coppa.
